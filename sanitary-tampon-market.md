<p><p>The sanitary tampon market is primarily divided into two types: conduit type and ductless sanitary tampons. Conduit type tampons feature a delivery system that allows menstrual flow to be absorbed through a conduit, ensuring a secure and efficient fit. In contrast, ductless sanitary tampons are designed without this system, relying on absorbent materials alone for menstrual protection. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, emphasizing comfort, reliability, and ease of use in menstrual hygiene products.</p></p>
