<p><p>The Dolby Atmos soundbar market is primarily categorized into two types: upward-firing soundbars and virtualized soundbars. Upward-firing soundbars feature speakers that direct sound towards the ceiling, creating a three-dimensional audio experience as sound reflects off surfaces. In contrast, virtualized soundbars utilize advanced algorithms to simulate surround sound without the need for additional speakers. Both types aim to enhance the home theater experience by providing immersive audio that elevates movie watching and gaming, appealing to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
